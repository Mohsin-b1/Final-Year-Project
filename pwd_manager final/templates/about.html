What is a password manager?



You have more passwords than you can remember? They all require you to have complex requirements. A minimum of 8 characters? So how are you supposed to remember so many strong and unique passwords? Well, you cannot it is not possible. But a password manager can remember them for you.

A password manager allows you to store and save your passwords, and access them anytime with the help of just one master password. Our password is simple and easy to use no need for daunting digital features. We keep our design simple and make it so anyone can use the password manager. All you need to do is click to add your credentials and then save them. Every time you need access to them just log in, copy, and paste away!
